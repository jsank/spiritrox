SpiritRox

SpiritRox aims to mimic the functionality that you may see on any particular ghost hunter or reality-based paranormal show. That is, the functionality of what is called a "Spirit Box." These devices are said to work because entities can "manipulate" electronic devices and select specific words from a dictionary in order to communicate with the living. Whether or not you believe this is up to you...but SpiritRox takes that to the next level by offering the same capabilities on your smartphone or tablet device. The important thing to remember when using this device is that it operates under the notion that these paranormal entities can indeed manipulate electronic signals in order to communicate.

You will notice that each word that is "chosen" by the spirit also offers a time-stamp and either the mark of low, medium, or high. These are added values that the spirit can utilize in order to show the strength of this communication. Generally speaking, the "high" category means the signal was in a very strong range and "low" means the opposite.

This is the initial release, so take it with a grain of salt. Updates are coming! Enjoy!
